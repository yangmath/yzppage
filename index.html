I received my PhD in 2022 from Mathematisches Institut at University of Göttingen, under the supervision of Professor Ingo Witt. Now, I am an assistant professor of mathematics at Yunnan Normal University. My research is mainly focused on Harmonic Analysis and Nonlinear Partial Differential Equations; Variational Methods; Analysis on homogeneous groups
